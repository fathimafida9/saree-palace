Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship all across India?▾
Yes! We deliver to all states and union territories across India. Free shipping is available on orders above Rs.1,499. International shipping is also available on request.
How do I return or exchange a saree?▾
You can return or exchange any product within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging. Contact us via email or WhatsApp to initiate a return.
Are your sarees 100% genuine and authentic?▾
Absolutely. All our silk sarees come with a Silk Mark certification. We source directly from GI-certified weaver clusters and artisan cooperatives across India.
Can I order custom blouse stitching?▾
Yes! We offer in-store blouse stitching at our Bengaluru store. For online customers, we have partnered tailors in major cities. Contact us on WhatsApp for more details.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?▾
Yes, we cater to bulk and wholesale orders for events, corporates, and retailers. Please send us your requirements via this contact form for a special quote.
How long does delivery take?▾
Standard delivery takes 3 to 5 business days. Express delivery (1 to 2 days) is available for Rs.99 extra. A tracking link will be sent once your order is shipped.
