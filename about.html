Our Story

Three decades of love for Indian textile heritage — from Hampankatta to homes across India.

Our Heritage

Born in Hampankatta,
Woven with Pride

In 1987, Saree Palace opened its doors in the heart of Hampankatta, Mangalore — with a simple belief: every woman deserves to be draped in the finest Indian silk and cotton, at honest prices.

What started as a single-room boutique has grown into one of the most trusted names in ethnic wear across coastal Karnataka. Today we carry over 5,000 sarees sourced directly from master weavers in Kanchipuram, Varanasi, Patan, and Chanderi.

Our mission remains unchanged — to preserve the art of handloom weaving while making authentic sarees accessible to every Indian household.
