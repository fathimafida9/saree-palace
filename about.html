About Saree Palace

35+ years of celebrating Indian craftsmanship, culture, and womanhood.

Est. 1987 · Bengaluru

Our Story – Where
Every Thread Tells a Tale

Founded in 1987 by Smt. Kamala Devi on the bustling streets of Bengaluru, Saree Palace began as a small but passionate boutique dedicated to bringing the finest handloom sarees from across India to a discerning clientele.

What started with a curated collection of Kanjivaram silks and Banarasi weaves soon grew into one of Karnataka's most beloved ethnic wear destinations. For over three decades, we have been the chosen destination for brides, families, and saree lovers alike.

Today, Saree Palace stocks over 5,000 products — from everyday cotton sarees to exquisite bridal collections — all sourced directly from master weavers and artisan clusters across India.
